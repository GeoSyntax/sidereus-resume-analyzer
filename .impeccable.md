## Design Context

### Users
Recruiters, interviewers, and assignment reviewers who need to upload one PDF resume, paste a job description, and quickly inspect whether the candidate is worth a deeper review.

### Brand Personality
Professional, restrained, and evidence-first. The interface should feel like a focused screening tool, not a marketing landing page or generic AI dashboard.

### Aesthetic Direction
Industrial-utilitarian light UI with dense but readable information, calm green accents, clear borders, compact controls, and strong evidence hierarchy. Avoid decorative gradients, oversized cards, dark SaaS styling, and promotional copy.

### Design Principles
- Put the workflow first: upload, job description, parse, match, inspect evidence.
- Show scoring evidence close to the score so reviewers can understand why a result was produced.
- Keep all copy short and operational; labels should sound like product controls, not advertising.
- Preserve predictable layout and keyboard-accessible form controls across desktop and mobile.
- Prefer simple borders, restrained spacing, and stable dimensions over decorative depth.
